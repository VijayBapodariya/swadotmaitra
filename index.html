About Us:

Established in 1995, Maitra Clinic provides comprehensive treatments for mental health. “Maitra” means ‘Sun’ or ‘Friendship’. It is a beacon of light for psychiatric patients and a trusted friend for those with mental health concerns. Mental health is just as important as one's physical health and yet, most of us are not mindful about the quality of our mental health.

At Maitra, we have more than 25 years of experience in treating mental health related issues. Our expertise lies not only in treatment of mental illnesses, but also in preventative mental health

OUR SERVICES

We are one of the very few, if not the only, private psychiatric practice in Maharashtra that offer 360-degree services for mental health. At our outpatient clinic, we are equipped to handle different aspects of mental health issues like diagnosis and comprehensive bio-psycho-social management. Our rehabilitation center - Saarth Sanmaan – provides preventative and rehabilitative mental health services.
