Swa.Tantraa - The Brain and Mind Gym is a unique program for cognitive, emotional & behavioral modification as well as for positive development of mental health through modern, enjoyable and highly scientific tools. It consists of a set of custom designed programs to address various preventitive mental health needs using an interactive, online platform for self-assessment, sharing and communication with our experts.

Many students, parents and professionals are using this facility for their development and well-being. We cater to individuals and institutions through our online subscription model.

The Swa.Tantraa initiative is run by Saarth Sanmaan.
