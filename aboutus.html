Our Team of Doctors and Practitioners

Dr. Animish Chavan

M.D., Psychiatry

Dr. Animish Chavan is an expert in the field of psychiatry, especially known for his comprehensive approach to treatment of mental illnesses. He is a skilled practitioner in administering brief psychotherapies, brain-mapping, biofeedback and psychiatric rehabilitation. He has also worked in the field of de-addiction since the beginning of his career and has authored various educational books on this topic, especially for teenagers.

He had been a part of the internationally acclaimed research program – COPSI (Community care for Patients of Schizophrenia in India) as a researcher and trainer of the amateur psychiatric health workers. He has addressed a large audience from various fields till date through local as well as national print and electronic media regarding Mental Health and Psychiatry. He is also associated with Library Movement, Constructivism in education and similar progressive movements in Maharashtra. Since 2014, he is associated with Telemedicine Society of India and is taking efforts to establish Maitra Tele-Mental Health Units in rural parts of Maharashtra. For the year 2015-2016, he has been the President of the Telemedicine Society of India, Maharashtra Chapter. He has established ‘SAARTH-SANMAAN’ Psychiatric Rehabilitation Center at Satara. This rehabilitation center is qualitatively offering need-based, tailor-made, humane and homely management. It offers cognitive remediation and vocational rehabilitation along with general rehabilitation tools. He has developed a unique program - ‘BRAIN AND MIND GYM’- at Satara which is for Cognitive and Emotional Mental Health Development with the use of many attractive, innovative tools. Many students, parents and professionals are using this facility for their development and well-being.

Dr. Vaishali Chavan

M.B.B.S., Diploma in Anaesthesiology

Dr. Vaishali Chavan is an anaesthesiologist and a certified practitioner in a number of mental health therapies such as Rational Emotive Behavioral Therapy (REBT), Narcotherapy and Grief Therapy. Clients at Maitra Clinic benefit from her rich experience in these areas, providing them with a holistic approach on their path to recovery.

Dr. Vaishali Chavan has been working at Maitra Clinic since 1997 as a Narco-therapist and Anaesthesiologist. She is a certified REBT practitioner (Albert Ellis Institute, New York). She completed primary & advanced certificate courses in Rational Emotive Behavioral Therapy (REBT) at Phadake center, Mumbai in 2001. In 2015, she completed primary & advanced certificate course at Albert Ellis Institute affiliated center- Morphic Minds, Nagpur. She also worked as a Senior Researcher and Trainer for health workers in the internationally acclaimed research project – COPSI (Community care for People with Schizophrenia in India). She is also trained in Grief therapy from Cipla Palliative Care in 2015. She is the founder & Vice–president of ‘NIRMITEE’ – A NGO for community psychiatry. Dr. Vaishali Chavan is the founder & Vice–President of ‘NIRMITEE’, the NGO for community psychiatry.

Prachi Mastoli

M.Sc.(Counselling and Psychotherapy)

Prachi Mastoli has been working in the field of mental health since 2002. She is an experienced Cognitive Behaviour Therapist and has worked with a number of patients, helping them overcome depression, anxiety and psychosis. Her special expertise is in cognitive rehabilitation and treating patients of psychosis with cognitive therapy. She has completed basic and advanced level training in cognitive behaviour therapy at Morphic Minds.

Prachi has completed Qualitative Research Methods training at SANGATH, Goa and has participated in COPSI (Community care for people with Schizophrenia in India) – the internationally acclaimed research project - as a Qualitative Researcher. Also she has trained Community Health Workers for the research project of COPSI. At Maitra, Prachi is the founder secretary of the NGO - NIRMITEE, working for Education, Empowerment and Treatment in the field of mental health. Prachi is the co-owner of the SAARTH-SANMAAN Psychiatric Rehabilitation Centre, Satara with Dr. Animish Chavan. At SAARTH-SANMAAN many psychiatric patients, especially with psychosis, are successfully and fully rehabilitated since 2011. Prachi also works with Dr. Animish Chavan, in his unique project Brain and Mind Gym, for the development and implementation of various programs.
